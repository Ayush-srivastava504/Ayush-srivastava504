## What I Do

**Data Analytics & Insights**

Conduct exploratory data analysis to uncover hidden patterns in complex datasets. Perform data cleaning and transformation to prepare datasets for analysis. Develop analytical frameworks for measuring performance and tracking KPIs, with custom metrics aligned to business objectives.

**Automated Reporting Workflows**

Design automated reporting workflows that deliver insights on schedule. Build KPI tracking dashboards enabling teams to monitor performance independently. Engineer data pipelines that eliminate manual reporting and create capacity for higher-value analytical work.

**Technical Execution**

Apply SQL for complex queries and data exploration, Python (Pandas, NumPy) for data manipulation and statistical analysis, Streamlit for rapid dashboard development, and DuckDB for lightweight, efficient analytical queries.

**Data-Driven Decision Making**

Translate business questions into actionable data insights. Perform comparative analysis across product lines, cohorts, and segments. Conduct root cause analysis to identify drivers of business performance and deliver clear recommendations supported by data visualizations.
